Source: Xinhua| 2020-05-11 22:32:05|Editor: huaxia
CHINA-WUHAN-COVID-19-CASES (CN)
A resident (R) takes vegetables purchased in group at the Sanmin residential community in Dongxihu District of Wuhan, central China's Hubei Province, May 11, 2020. Wuhan reported a total of six new confirmed COVID-19 cases on Saturday and Sunday. All the patients are from the Sanmin residential community. Residents of the Sanmin residential community finished testing Monday.
