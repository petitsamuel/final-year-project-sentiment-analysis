One thing is for certain, Christmas 2020 will be very different from previous years. Covid-19 has changed so many aspects of our daily lives, but it doesn’t have to stop us from having a magical holiday season. Consider “A Covid-19 Christmas – 101 Ways to Make Christmas 2020 Magical” your roadmap to joy during the merriest season of them all. Inside this book you’ll find fun, safe alternatives to many traditional holiday activities that have been canceled in 2020. Use this book as a guide to let love, laughter, and happiness prevail during the Christmas season. I’ve included many festivities that can be enjoyed from the comfort of your own home and are sure to bring joy to the entire family. This book was written to inspire others to have the best Christmas ever and renew holiday spirit that may have been lost due to the global pandemic.
